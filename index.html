Introduction

In YU-GI-OH! Forbidden Memories, cards can be redeemed/purchased with STARCHIP when the correct PASSWORD is entered in the PASSWORD screen on the TITLE menu.

You will be prompted with the STARCHIP cost of the card upon successful password entry and you can then confirm whether you want to redeem the specific card.

You can get 1 to 5 STARCHIP from winning duel in both CAMPAIGN and FREE DUEL mode; and Each card can only be redemmed/purchased once under a single DUELIST CODE.

Instead of submitting 3 FAQs, I am revising this guide into 3 sections, one by STARCHIP, one by CARD TYPE ans the final on by CARD NUMBER for easy reference.


Cheers :)


Tips on Using This Guide

You can use the SEARCH feature on you browser or your text editor (if you are downloading this guide) and type in the CARD NUMBER or CARD TITLE for an easy lookup. By the way, 0 starchip means not redeemable.
